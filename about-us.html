About Us

Sterling Academy

Sterling Academy is a specialized Logistics, Supply Chain, and Export Import Management business-training institute, formed with the purpose of educating students, executives, and entrepreneurs. We are a trust with noble objectives: to create the finest resources in this sector and shape tomorrow’s market leaders. We have successfully trained over 350+ students with a placement record of 87% in leading companies across India. Additionally, some of our students, with a solid foundation of domestic experience, have transitioned to international roles in the UAE, Qatar, and Saudi Arabia, leveraging their knowledge and expertise gained in India. We offer courses in Logistics, Freight Forwarding, Export Import Management, and Export. With Sterling Academy, you'll gain the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in the dynamic world of international trade.In todayʼs interconnected world, expertise in export/import logistics opens doors to countless opportunities. By deepening your understanding, you not only enhance your professional capabilities but also position yourself as a valuable asset in a rapidly evolving global industry.
